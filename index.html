About Low's Adventure 2

Low's Adventure 2 is a thrilling 2D platformer where you guide your character through challenging levels filled with obstacles, enemies, and collectibles. Jump, dash, and navigate through 32 exciting levels, collect coins to unlock the exit, and avoid dangerous obstacles like falling thorns, fixed spikes, cannon fire, and teal crawlers.

Use power-ups like umbrellas to glide on your jumps and wings to fly across large gaps. Test your skills in boss battles on stages 26 and 32!

How To Play Low's Adventure 2

Use your keyboard arrow keys to move left or right and the up arrow key to jump. Collect all the coins to activate the level exit trophy, then reach the trophy to complete the level.

Avoid falling off platforms and don't get hit by enemies or obstacles. Collect power-ups like umbrellas for better control during jumps and wings for temporary flight ability.
